James Hughes is one of the leading clarinet students in The Midlands; despite his age, James is a calm and confident musician.

Based in the county town of Warwick, he is available for events anywhere in Warwickshire, or the surrounding area.

  • James studies clarinet & cello at the Royal Birmingham Conservatoire, and regularly performs with their Junior String & Woodwind Orchestras.
  • James performs, on the clarinet, with the National Children's Orchestra - he will be part of the “Under 11” orchestra during 2018.
  • James won the Peter Jarvis Trophy, awarded to the “most promising” clarinettist at the Leamington Spa Competitive Festival, in both 2016 & 2017.
  • James is a music scholar at Warwick School, where he leads and plays in a wide range of groups & orchestras.

If you are interested in contacting James to discuss opportunities, please email contact@warwickclarinet.co.uk, and we will get back to you as soon as we can.
